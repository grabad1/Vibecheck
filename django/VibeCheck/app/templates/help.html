Getting Started

Click on the "Sign up" button on the homepage or pricing page. Fill in your username, email, and password, then click "Create Account". You'll be ready to start creating collabs right away!

A collab is a collaborative playlist where you and your friends can add songs together. It's perfect for parties, road trips, or just sharing music with friends!

Yes! VibeCheck offers a free plan where you can create up to 5 collabs, invite up to 3 friends per collab, and add up to 10 songs per collab. For unlimited features, check out our Premium plan for just $5/month.

Managing Collabs

From your dashboard, click on "Create New Collab" button. Give your collab a name, choose whether it's public or private, and start inviting friends! You can customize it further after creation.

Open your collab and look for the "Invite Friends" or "Add Members" button. You can invite friends by their username or share the collab link with them. They'll receive a notification and can join instantly!

Public collabs can be discovered by anyone on VibeCheck and appear in the trending section. Private collabs are only accessible to people you specifically invite - perfect for personal playlists with close friends!

Yes! As the collab creator, you can manage members. Go to your collab settings, find the member you want to remove, and click the remove button next to their name.

Adding Songs

Open your collab and click "Add Song". You can search for songs by name, artist, or album, or paste a Spotify link directly. Each song you add will be linked to its Spotify page so everyone can listen!

No, you don't need a Spotify account to use VibeCheck! However, when you click on a song, it will open in Spotify (web or app), where you might need an account to listen. VibeCheck just uses Spotify links to organize your music.

Currently, VibeCheck only supports Spotify links. We chose Spotify because of its extensive library and easy-to-share links. Support for other platforms may come in future updates!

Yes! You can remove any song you added. If you're the collab creator, you can also remove songs added by other members. Just click the delete/remove icon next to the song.

Premium Features

Premium unlocks unlimited everything! Create unlimited collabs, invite as many friends as you want, and add unlimited songs to each collab. No restrictions, just pure vibing with your music community.

VibeCheck Premium is just $5 per month. You can upgrade anytime from the pricing page and start enjoying unlimited features immediately!

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your Premium benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll return to the free plan limits.

Don't worry - you won't lose any data! If you have more than 5 collabs, you'll still be able to view all of them, but you won't be able to create new ones until you're back under the limit. Same goes for songs and members in each collab.

Account & Settings

Go to your account settings, click on "Security" or "Change Password", enter your current password, then your new password twice to confirm. Make sure to use a strong password!

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

Yes! Go to your account settings and look for the "Profile" section. You can update your username there. Keep in mind that your friends will see your new username in all shared collabs.

We're sad to see you go! Go to account settings and look for "Delete Account" at the bottom. This action is permanent and will delete all your collabs, songs, and data. Make sure to save anything important first!

Troubleshooting

Make sure you're copying the full Spotify link (it should start with "https://open.spotify.com/"). The link should point directly to a track. If you're still having issues, try searching for the song by name instead of pasting a link.

Check your notifications to see if you need to accept the invitation first. If you already accepted, try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, the collab might have been deleted by its creator.

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then refresh the page. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If problems continue, try using a different browser or device. You can also check if other users are experiencing issues by contacting support.

We appreciate bug reports! Send an email to support@vibecheck.com with details about the issue, including what you were trying to do, what happened, and what browser/device you're using. Screenshots are super helpful too!
